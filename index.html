About

A little about me myself and I: My name is Mindaugas, or just MJ for short. I am Lithuanian but currently reside in England, London, where I’d like to work with people of similar interests. Even though I seemingly lack the experience that everyone so desires, I have yet to come across a problem that I couldn’t solve (I love solving problems). Not so long ago I decided that aspects of programming are really much more towards what I prefer, I mean come on, it’s just full of difficult problems and solutions to them, what’s not to love? Before that I mainly dealt with CMSs to make simple pages for my own or others to use. My most used one is WordPress, primarily because it’s administration is easily taught to people with no programming knowledge. Most of my programming is self taught, with my farourite method of learning – coming up with an idea and doing it. Even though I studied electronic business management in college, we didn’t really focus much on programing it self, primarally on managing a business online. I did, how ever, aquire my basic knowledge there and it did help my a whole lot. As for now, I work two jobs, unrelated to my what I want to do in life, and barely have any time to work on my web site. Still I will push through, as I know what I want to do. Beginings are always the most difficult part, but if they weren’t where would the fun be hm?
